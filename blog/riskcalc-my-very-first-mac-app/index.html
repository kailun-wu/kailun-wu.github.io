RiskCalc: My Very First Mac App

Jan 2nd, 2015

Download RiskCalc v0.5 for Yosemite

RiskCalc is a risk management app that quickly calculate VaR, LGD, histogram and hedge for a trading book of securities. It’s my final project for the Financial Software course at NYU and also my first ever Cocoa app.
